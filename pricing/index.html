Pricing FAQ

What tier is right for me?

Start with an Assessment to understand your opportunities. Most companies then move to Implementation, followed by a Retainer for ongoing support.

What are acceptance criteria?

Measurable targets that must be met before project sign-off: ingest ≥98%, health compute <2 min, agent uptime >99.9%.

Can I switch between tiers?

Yes, you can upgrade or modify your retainer tier anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes, annual commitments receive 15% discount on retainer pricing.
