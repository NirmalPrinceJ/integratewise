Company Overview

IntegrateWise is a platform-agnostic enterprise integration and AI automation company built for Customer Success (CSM) operations. We normalize operational data once (Atlas Spine on PostgreSQL) and orchestrate workflows with n8n, then render read-only views in the tools teams already use.

Notion Coda Airtable Asana ClickUp Monday Google Sheets

Our 7-agent suite delivers renewal forecasting, health scoring, integration design, data quality, security governance, template automation, and margin-aware deal decisions—without vendor lock-in.

Become the default "integration + AI layer" for SaaS CSM teams in APAC, proving measurable outcomes in 90 days and scaling globally through platform- and tool-agnostic architecture.
