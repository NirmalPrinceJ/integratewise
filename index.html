FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What exactly is IntegrateWise?

IntegrateWise is a Customer Success Data Platform that does three things: (1) unifies your customer data from CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot into one source of truth, (2) uses AI agents to predict churn 30+ days early, and (3) pushes health scores and alerts to the tools your team already uses‚ÄîNotion, Airtable, Sheets, etc.

How is this different from Gainsight or other CS platforms?

Unlike monolithic CS platforms, IntegrateWise is platform-agnostic. We don't force you into our UI‚Äîwe normalize your data once, then render it wherever your team works. Plus, our AI agents predict churn autonomously, not just via rules you configure. And we deploy in 2 weeks, not 6 months.

What CRMs and tools do you integrate with?

We ingest from Salesforce, HubSpot, and Microsoft Dynamics. Outputs render to Notion, Airtable, Coda, Google Sheets, Asana, ClickUp, and Monday. Custom integrations available for Scale tier customers.

How long does implementation take?

Most customers see initial value in 2 weeks with our MVP deployment‚Äîthat includes CRM connection, canonical schema setup, and basic health scoring. Full implementation with AI agents and custom calibration takes 4-8 weeks. Measurable ROI documented within 90 days.

How do the AI agents predict churn?

Our ChurnShield agent analyzes patterns across usage data, engagement signals, stakeholder changes, and historical outcomes. It identifies risk signals 30+ days before renewal‚Äîgiving your team time to intervene. Accuracy target: 85%+ with 15-25% churn reduction.

What's the pricing model?

We offer three SaaS tiers: Starter ($99/mo) for small teams up to 500 accounts, Growth ($499/mo) for mid-market with AI agents, and Scale ($1,499/mo) for unlimited integrations and dedicated support. Enterprise and outcome-tied pricing available.

Is our data secure?

Yes. We're SOC 2 compliant with SSL encryption, 30-day data retention, and full audit trails. Secrets are rotated automatically via our VaultGuard agent. Your CRM data stays in PostgreSQL with read-only views pushed to destination tools.

Do we need engineering resources to implement?

No engineering required for standard deployments. We handle all integrations via OAuth connectors. Your CS Ops or RevOps team can manage the platform. Enterprise customers with custom requirements may need minimal IT involvement for SSO or custom adapters.
