Measurement Methodology

Applies to churn reduction, efficiency gains, and ROI claims.

Baselines

Use trailing 90-day churn %, renewal rate, and CSM hours per account before go-live. Lock baselines before first production run.

Windows

Measure monthly with quarterly rollups; time-to-value tracked at 90 days from first successful health compute.

Exclusions

Remove accounts with contract changes >50%, mergers, or outages >24h unrelated to platform.

Attribution

Churn reduction attributed when risk flags precede renewal by ≥30 days; efficiency credited when playbooks replace manual tickets.

ROI Formula

ROI = (Retained Revenue + Efficiency Savings − Fees) / Fees. Efficiency hours valued at loaded CSM rate.
