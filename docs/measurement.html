Measurement

Methodology

Baselines

Set pre-go-live churn, expansion, and efficiency baselines using trailing 2–4 quarter data. Lock baselines at executive readout.

Windows

Primary window: 90 days post go-live. Secondary windows: 180 and 365 days for longitudinal tracking.

Exclusions

Exclude deals with contractual churn (M&A, bankruptcy), out-of-scope products, and customers without adapter coverage.

Attribution

Risk saves tied to ChurnShield tasks; revenue lifts tied to DealDesk workflows; efficiency gains tied to TemplateForge adoption and CSM hours logged.

ROI Formula

ROI = (Retained revenue + Expansion + Efficiency savings − Fees) / Fees. Efficiency savings use blended CSM hourly rate agreed in assessment.
