Agent Spec

TemplateForge

Playbooks and dashboards templated per workspace.

Inputs

workspace preferences, role matrix, metrics dictionary, approved playbooks, dashboard layouts.

Outputs

templated assets delivered to Notion/Airtable/Asana/ClickUp/Monday/Sheets with change logs and owner.

Webhooks

template.published and template.updated events with workspace_id, asset_uri, checksum, owner.

SLAs

Generation <60s for standard templates; adoption tracking available within 15 minutes; uptime >99.9%.

Versioning

Templates versioned per workspace; major when layout/fields change; minor for copy or metric adjustments.
