Agent Spec

DealDesk

Renewal and expansion orchestration.

Inputs

accounts, contracts, forecast data, stakeholder map, playbook definitions, pricing guardrails.

Outputs

deal_workflow (jsonb) with tasks, owners, due dates, and approvals; updates to health_scores when milestones met.

Webhooks

deal.stage_changed and approval.requested with deal_id, stage, owner, approvals_required, and SLA timers.

SLAs

Stage transitions processed <60s; approval fan-out in <30s; uptime >99.9%; audit retention 180 days.

Versioning

Playbook schema versioned; major when stage definitions change; minor when owners or SLAs shift.
